Mission About

Together for a Cleaner, Healthier Planet

Our mission is to combat climate change and protect the environment through education, research, innovation, and action — empowering individuals and organizations to create sustainable solutions, supporting startups and projects that advance environmental progress, and advocating for policies that promote a cleaner, healthier planet.

About & Vision

PlanetRise Foundation is a nonprofit focused on real climate action and practical solutions. Our vision is simple: operate across the globe so communities everywhere can access tools, knowledge, and support to build a sustainable future.

We currently operate in the United States and Taiwan and plan to expand our work internationally.
